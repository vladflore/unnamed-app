Barbell Squat

Strength Legs Barbell
How to Perform
  1. Stand with feet shoulder-width apart.
  2. Place barbell securely on upper traps.
  3. Brace your core and maintain posture.
  4. Lower hips until thighs are parallel to floor.
  5. Push through heels to return to standing.
Target Muscle Groups
  • Primary: Quads, Glutes
  • Secondary: Core, Hamstrings
Key Cues
  • ✔ Keep knees in line with toes
  • ✔ Maintain a neutral spine
  • ✔ Control the descent and push through heels
Muscle Groups Quads, Glutes, Core
Equipment Barbell, Rack
Routine 5×5 • 90s rest • Tempo 2-1-1
